Anxiety

When your mind won’t slow down

Dr. Sarah West
• Jan 09, 2026 • 7 min read

Racing thoughts can be exhausting and overwhelming. It feels like your mind is running a marathon without a finish line. If you're struggling with anxiety, know that you're not alone and there are ways to find relief.

1. Practice Mindfulness

Mindfulness is the practice of bringing your attention to the present moment without judgment. It helps you detach from racing thoughts and cultivate a sense of calm.

2. Limit Stimulants

Caffeine and sugar can exacerbate anxiety symptoms. Pay attention to how your body reacts to these substances and limit your intake if necessary.

3. Seek Professional Help

If anxiety is interfering with your daily life, consider speaking with a mental health professional. Therapy and medication can be effective tools for managing anxiety.

"You don't have to control your thoughts. You just have to stop letting them control you." - Dan Millman

Remember, healing takes time. Be patient with yourself and celebrate small victories along the way.
