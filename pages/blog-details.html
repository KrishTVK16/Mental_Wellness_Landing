5 Ways to De-stress in Under 5 Minutes

Dr. Sarah West
• Jan 20, 2026 • 5 min read

Stress is an inevitable part of modern life, but it doesn't have to control you. Research shows that micro-breaks—short pauses taken throughout the day—can significantly lower cortisol levels and reset your nervous system.

1. Box Breathing

This technique is used by Navy SEALs to stay calm under pressure. Breathe in for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, and hold for 4. Repeat this cycle four times.

2. The 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

Engage your senses to bring yourself back to the present moment:

  • 5 things you see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste

3. Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense each muscle group for 5 seconds, then release. Start from your toes and work your way up to your head. Feel the tension leaving your body.

"The greatest weapon against stress is our ability to choose one thought over another." - William James

Incorporating these simple exercises into your daily routine can make a massive difference in your long-term mental health. Remember, peace is a practice, not a destination.
